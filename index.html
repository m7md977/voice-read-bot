Three steps

Up and running in a minute

No dashboards, no config files. Add the bot, hop in a voice channel, and start typing.

1

Add the bot

Invite Voice Read Bot to your server with a couple of clicks.

2

Run /read start

Join a voice channel, then run the command in the text channel you want read.

3

Just type

Every new message is spoken aloud — prefixed with the sender's name.

What it does

Built for real conversations

Powered by ElevenLabs, tuned to read chat the way people actually talk.

English & Arabic voices

A whole cast of ElevenLabs voices — US & UK English, plus Modern Standard and Gulf Arabic. Pick the one that fits your server.

🇺🇸 English🇬🇧 British🇸🇦 عربي فصحى🇦🇪 خليجي

Natural, clear speech

High-quality synthesis that sounds like a person reading — not a robot. Choose a faster or higher-quality model per session.

Your voice, your model

Set a default, or switch voice and model on the fly with command options.

Emoji, spoken

Emojis become words — 🔥 reads as “fire” instead of being skipped.

Clean reading

Links, mentions, and markdown are stripped so they don't get read aloud.

Leaves on its own

Auto-disconnects after a channel sits empty, so it never lingers.

The cast

Sixteen voices, two languages

A sample of who can read your chat. Hover to pause.

One command

Everything runs through /read

Start, stop, pause, resume, skip, clear the queue, or check status — all from a single slash command.

/read start /read pause /read resume /read skip /read clear /read status /read stop
